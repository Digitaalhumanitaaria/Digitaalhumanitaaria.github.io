Activities of the Society

For its goals the society has contributed in the following spheres

  • Helps with the organization of the annual Digital Humanities Conferences in Estonia.
    • These events have been happening annually since 2013. See links here 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016. The society is helping with organization since 2016
  • Constructing a wiki on Digital Humanities topics in Estonia
    • The wiki is maintained by Keeleleek.
  • Organizes seminars to share our experiences in using software for humanities research
    • The first seminar took place on June 10, 2016. A main page for them has not been organized.
  • Monitor the presence of digital humanities in the media
    • We're gathering a link collection on relevant local articles.
